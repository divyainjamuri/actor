Sai Pallavi(Actress)

Sai Pallavi Senthamarai Kannan (born 9 May 1992) known mononymously as Sai Pallavi, is an Indian actress and dancer who works primarily in Tamil, Telugu and Malayalam films. She is regarded as one of the most accomplished actresses in South Indian cinema. She is the recipient of numerous accolades including four Filmfare Awards South and two South Indian International Movie Awards. Her performance was ranked by Film Companion amongst 100 Greatest Performances of the Decade. She was featured by Forbes magazine as one of India's 30 under 30 in 2020.

Early Life

Sai Pallavi was born on 9 May 1992 in a Badaga Hindu family in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Her hometown is Kotagiri in the Nilgiris district, Tamil Nadu.Born to Senthamarai Kannan and Radha, She has a younger sister, Pooja Kannan, who has also worked as an actress. She was raised and educated in Coimbatore, doing her schooling at Avila Convent School. Despite completing her medical studies in 2016 from the Tbilisi State Medical University which is recognised by Medical Council of India, she has not yet registered as a medical practitioner in India.

Profession and Passion

While the actress has made a massive name for herself in the South Indian cinema, Pallavi would have leaned towards a completely different profession if it wasn't for films. By training, Sai Pallavi is a doctor. The actress completed her MBBS studies from the Tbilisi State Medical University. She is an MBBS in the field of education. She completed her MBBS in the year 2016 . But her interest was a drag in movies and dance so she did not do the job and went for the movie. She is a talented South Indian actress and dancer who rose to fame with her performance in the 2015 film Premam. At an early age, Sai Pallavi was extremely passionate about dance and dreamed of becoming a classical dancer. She attended Hindustan Arts and Science College in Chennai, where she learned Bharatnatyam through private tuition for six years, subsequently completing her course at Kalakshetra Foundation in Thiruvanmiyur Pattinapakkam. Her passion for dance also led her to be part of many theatre productions including Kalidas’s ‘Vikramorvasiyam’ as Urvashi, which enhanced her performing skills further. Ballet classes have been an integral part of her life since a tender age, due to which she has become one of the most admired dancers among moviegoers today. Apart from being trained in dance, Sai Pallavi is also proficient at other forms such as aerobics, swimming, and basketball and throws discus competitively too! Despite such varied talents she remains focused on cinema, appearing in some notable movies like Diya (2018) & Maari 2 (2018).
